What is DataVerse?
DataVerse is an all-in-one web application designed to simplify and enhance your data analysis and visualization experience. Whether you're working with sales data, stock inventories, or any other dataset, DataVerse provides powerful tools to transform raw data into actionable insights. With features like data analysis, data visualizations, and stock trend predictions, DataVerse is a go-to platform for analysts, business professionals, and anyone who wants to make data-driven decisions. Additionally, our recommendation feature can analyze your sales or stock data to highlight top-selling products, optimize restocking strategies, and suggest budget-friendly stock recommendations. Unlock the full potential of your data with DataVerse.

How to Use?

In DataVerse, Analysis feature is divided into two key components: Sales Analysis and Stock Analysis, each tailored to deliver targeted insights and enhance your data-driven decision-making.

Sales Analysisüìä

To utilize the Sales Analysis feature, your dataset must include the following columns:

ID Sale ID.
Date The date of the sale.
Product_Name The name of the product sold.
Category The category of the product sold.
Payment_Mode Mode of payment made by customer.
Quantity The number of units sold.
Cost_Price The cost price per unit of the product.
Selling_Price The selling price per unit of the product.
Amount The amount of product before discount.
Discount The discount applied to the product, if any.
Net_Amount The net amount after discount.

Stock Analysisüìä

For the Stock Analysis feature, your dataset should include the following columns:

Date The date of the stock data.
Script The stock ticker symbol or script name.
Open The opening price of the stock for the given date.
Close The closing price of the stock for the given date.
High The highest price reached by the stock on that date.
Low The lowest price reached by the stock on that date.

If your dataset doesn't align with the required schema, you can either modify it to include these specific columns or simply rename the existing columns to fit the required format. Following these guidelines will ensure that the Analysis feature functions correctly.
